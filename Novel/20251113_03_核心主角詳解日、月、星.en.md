# Core Protagonists Explained: Sun, Moon, and Star

These three protagonists are the trinity of "The Creation Engine Project," inseparable and complementary. They represent the "Will," "Faith," and "Wisdom" necessary to start a revolution.

### Backstory: The "Young Master" in the House's Shadow

In the end, Gao Sheng's father was publicly executed.

In that moment, Gao Sheng's "Id" learned a bloody lesson: "Will," without "probability" to back it up, is just a foolish form of suicide.

#### Growth: Playing the "Model Corrupt Official"

The "Ministry of Integration" needed a new "white glove" to manage the chaotic "Hundred-Yue Ports"â€”they needed someone to handle the "dirty" business they wouldn't touch, and to "use criminals to control criminals."

They chose Gao Sheng, the "traitor's" son.

Gao Sheng's "Ego" made the most perfect "calculation." He needed to survive, and he needed "capital." He completely abandoned his father's "nobility" and put on a "greasy, greedy, non-threatening" mask. He became the "Presidium's" favorite "model corrupt official"â€”a useless "young master" who only cared about debauchery and money.

- **Money (Capital)**: The "fuel" to start any plan.

- **Connections (Network)**: The casino was a crossroads for all walks of life. He used it to build an underground intelligence network that spanned "The Harmony" and even the "Gray Mirror Alliance."

#### The Superego's Hidden Goal:

He never forgot his father's death. His "Superego"â€”the deeply hidden rebelâ€”hated this "prison of consciousness." He was waiting, using his actuary's brain, for a "Wild Card" to appear. A variable that could shift his "zero-win" hand to "not zero."

## 4. The Trinity: The Interaction of Sun, Moon, and Star

#### Phase One: The Meeting (Sun and Moon)

Gao Sheng (Sun) discovers Isa/Ying Yue (Moon) in his black market fighting ring. He (the gambling king) doesn't understand her "martial arts," but he understands her "will." He sees her as his "ultimate trump card," a "non-probabilistic" being, and hides her away, making her his deepest secret.

#### Phase Two: The Collision (Star and Moon)

Xing Wangchen (Star) wanders to the "Hundred-Yue Ports" and sings his "encrypted" song (about the "Consortium" and the "Frost") outside Gao Sheng's casino.

Gao Sheng (the gambling king) hears the "rebellion" in the song and secretly brings him in.
